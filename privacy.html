Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  |  We keep this simple and clear.

The short version: We collect only what we need, we don't sell your data, and we use Supabase (hosted in the EU/US) to securely store your account information. Your mental health information stays private.

1. What We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address and password (encrypted)
  • Your name (if provided during sign-up)
  • Articles you save to your reading list
  • Basic usage data (pages visited, time on site) via anonymous analytics

We do not collect sensitive health or therapy session content. Expert sessions are arranged directly between you and the professional.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To save your reading list and preferences
  • To send you transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset)
  • To improve the platform through anonymous analytics

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.
