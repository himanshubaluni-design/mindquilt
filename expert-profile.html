About

Dr. Ananya Sharma is a licensed clinical psychologist with over 12 years of experience working with individuals dealing with anxiety, OCD, and trauma. She obtained her PhD from NIMHANS and has completed additional training in EMDR and mindfulness-based cognitive therapy.

Her approach combines evidence-based techniques with warmth and empathy, creating a safe space where clients feel truly heard. She believes in empowering her clients to develop lasting coping skills, not just managing symptoms.

Therapeutic Approach

Dr. Sharma uses an integrative approach, drawing from Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), and mindfulness techniques. Sessions are structured yet flexible, tailored to each individual's unique needs and pace.
